About Aussie Knee Coach

Clear, practical knee coaching without the guesswork.

A lot of people dealing with knee issues end up stuck between rest, random exercises, flare-ups, frustration, and second-guessing what to do next.

What’s often missing isn’t effort. It’s structure, progression, and support.

Aussie Knee Coach was built to help suitable active adults follow a clear plan, build confidence, and make real progress with their knee.

FAQ

Common questions

Who is this for?

Suitable active adults dealing with ACL rehab, patellar tendon pain, patellofemoral pain, or ongoing knee issues.

How do calls work?

Calls can be done via Zoom or WhatsApp depending on client preference.

What app do you use?

Programs are delivered through Everfit.

Do I need to live in Australia?

No. The service is online and can suit clients in different locations, depending on fit and scope.

Do you diagnose injuries?

No. Aussie Knee Coach provides structured online coaching and support for suitable clients. It does not replace medical assessment, diagnosis, or emergency care.

Why 12 weeks?

Real progress usually takes consistency over time. A 12-week block gives enough time to build strength, confidence, and momentum.
