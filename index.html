About Aussie Knee Coach

Clear, practical knee coaching without the guesswork.

Aussie Knee Coach was built to help active adults get structured, practical support for ongoing knee issues without bouncing between random exercises or trying to work it out alone.

With qualifications in fitness, human movement, secondary education, and 16 years of experience dealing with knee issues, the goal is simple: help suitable clients build strength, confidence, and function through a clear online coaching process.

What makes this different

  • Knees-only focus
  • Personalised exercise programming
  • Ongoing progression, not random advice
  • Support through WhatsApp or Zoom
  • Built for motivated active adults

Who It's For

FAQ

Common questions

Who is this program for?

The program is designed for suitable active adults dealing with issues such as ACL rehab, patellar tendon pain, or patellofemoral pain who want structured online coaching and support.

Do I need to live in Australia?

No. Online coaching can be delivered remotely, subject to client suitability and service scope.

How does communication work?

Coaching is delivered through TrueCoach, with communication and support via WhatsApp and calls through Zoom or WhatsApp depending on client preference.

Why is the program 12 weeks?

Real progress usually takes consistency over time. A 12-week block gives enough structure to build strength, confidence, and momentum.

Do you diagnose injuries?

No. Aussie Knee Coach provides structured coaching and exercise support for suitable clients. It does not replace medical assessment, diagnosis, or emergency care.
